Our Origin

In 2022, after decades working inside law firms and advising them from the outside, we saw the same pattern everywhere: brilliant legal minds losing hours to financial administration, partners anxious about cash flow despite full order books, and millions locked up in inefficient processes.

The technology existed to solve this. The data was there. But nobody had connected the dots in a way that made sense for legal. So we did.

Today, we're working with 127 firms across the UK, and we're just getting started. Our goal: put £4.4 billion back into the legal sector by 2028.
