Step 3: Data Migration Strategy

One of the biggest concerns firms have is data migration. Here's what you need to know:

🗃️ What Data Can Be Migrated

✅ Easily Migrated

  • Client contact information
  • Matter details and descriptions
  • Invoice and payment history
  • Time recording data
  • Basic document metadata

⚠️ Partially Migrated

  • Contract analysis results (summary only)
  • Document templates (reformatted)
  • Workflow configurations (rebuilt)
  • Custom reports (recreated)

❌ Not Needed

  • Ayora.ai-specific AI models
  • Complex workflow automations
  • US-specific compliance data
  • System configuration files

🎯 What Yieldly Needs

Essential for Cash Flow Analysis

  • Client payment history (last 24 months)
  • Invoice data and payment terms
  • Work-in-progress information
  • Matter start and completion dates
  • Fee earner time allocation

Optional for Enhanced Features

  • Client industry classifications
  • Matter type categorisation
  • Historical cash flow patterns
  • Seasonal business variations
